Scorpio (October 23 - November 22) Deep and passionate Scorpio is a mystery to most! This sign is directly associated with the underworld, so they’re right at home in the darkest of places. But it’s not about dwelling here forever. Scorpio the Scorpion is all about transformation, and their ultimate goal is to bring all that’s hidden into the light.






Sagittarius (November 22 - December 21) Adventurous, optimistic, and lucky Sagittarius aims high and shoots for the stars! Ruled by Jupiter, the planet of growth and opportunity, this sign of the Archer is an eternal student, looking for enlightenment through new ideas, people, and places. Carefree Sagittarius has a positive and outgoing personality that makes them the life of any party.






Capricorn (December 22 - January 19) Slow and steady, cool and calculating, Capricorn is a hard-working and successful force to be reckoned with! The Mountain Goat has a destination in mind and will stop at nothing to reach its goal. Caring, conservative Capricorn is a great leader and a dedicated provider.
