About Libra:

Libra
With a symbol of the scales in the zodiac,

the sign of Libra is the sign of justice, balance and harmony.

That is why Libra's are natural peacekeepers.


Traits of Libra:



In the game of love, Libras are a bundle of energy,

romantic and loyal to the core. The Scales need to be on the lookout for their lower backs, though,

which tend to shoulder any burdens they may be carrying. The great strength of the Libra-born

is their quest for fairness, peace and harmony.
