About


Discover the hidden gems of the Irish countryside. From the rolling hills of Ulster to the rugged Atlantic coastline of the west, Tourists Ireland is a one-stop-shop for the most breath-taking places to visit and the finest places to indulge yourself in some delicious Irish food. Also included are some of the popular attractions of Dublin, Ireland's laid-back, eclectic capital city.

Ireland has something to offer to everyone. Children can be entertained in many ways such as Titantic Museum, Lough Key Forest Park and Tayto Park. Ireland is also home to a multitude of historical sites such as Kilmainham Gaol, Blarney Castle and the Rock of Cashel. Ireland's rich heritage and culture can be showcased by a trip to the west to places like Galway, Achill Island and Ennis. The stunning scenery of the Burren, Connemara and Dun Aonghasa fill visitors with wonder and provide many poignant moments, especially at dawn or sunset.
