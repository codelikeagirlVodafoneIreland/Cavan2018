  • Canoeing- River Erne, Belturbet

  • Bike Hire- Portruan, Belturbet

  • Gymnasium- Belturbet fitness centre

  • Angling- Belturbet town

  • Turbet Island- Belturbet town

  • Football- Rory O'Moore's park

  • Music- Comhaltas, Belturbet
